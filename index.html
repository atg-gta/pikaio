Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers.

What kind of photos do I need to provide?

For best results, provide 30-60 clear photos of yourself in various lighting, angles, and backgrounds. Good quality selfies and social photos work perfectly. The more variety, the more realistic and versatile your results.

How is my data handled?

Your privacy is our priority. Your photos are used only to create your custom AI model and are deleted permanently after your project is complete. They are never shared or used for any other purpose.

What if I'm not happy with the results?

Your satisfaction is key. Our Pro and Enterprise packages include revision rounds where we'll work with you on your feedback to refine the images until they're perfect.

How long does it take?

Expect your first proof set within 1-2 business days of sending your photos. If your package includes revisions, each round typically takes 2-3 extra business days.

Do I get full ownership and commercial rights?

Yes! For our 'Ultimate Identity' package (for individuals) and all 'Business' packages, you receive full commercial usage rights. Other packages include a personal usage license.

Is this ethical?

Our service is about enhancement, not deception. We require you to have the rights to the photos you provide and will not create 'deepfakes' or images of other individuals without consent.

Can you create photos in any style or location?

Absolutely. Our AI can place you in virtually any setting you can imagine—from a professional office to a fantasy landscape. Just describe your vision, and we'll bring it to life with incredible detail and realism.

How do revisions work?

For packages that include revisions, you'll receive a gallery of images to review. You can provide specific feedback on the ones you'd like adjusted, and our team will work on refining them. This collaborative process ensures the final images meet your expectations.

Have more questions?

Our team is ready to help. Reach out and we'll be happy to answer any questions you have about our process or technology.
