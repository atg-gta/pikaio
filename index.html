Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers.

What kind of photos do I need to provide?

For best results, provide 30-60 clear photos of yourself in various lighting, angles, and backgrounds. Good quality selfies and social photos work perfectly. These don't need to be professional; just everyday social photos are perfect. The more variety, the more realistic and versatile your results.

How are my photos handled?

Your privacy matters. Every image you send is stored in a secure, private folder. We use them only to build your custom AI model and delete them permanently after the project is complete. Nothing is shared or reused EVER.

What if I'm not happy with the results?

Our process is highly collaborative. We provide a proof gallery for you to review. Our Pro and Premium packages include revision rounds where we'll work with you on your feedback to refine the prompts and generate new images that perfectly match your vision. Your satisfaction is key.

How long does it take?

Expect your first proof set within 1-2 business days of sending your photos. If your package includes revisions, each round typically takes 2-3 extra business days. Fast, clear communication helps us move even quicker.

Do I get full ownership and commercial rights?

Yes! For our 'Ultimate Identity' package (for individuals) and all 'Business' packages, you receive full commercial usage rights. 'Personal Refresh' and 'Visionary Profile' packages include a personal usage license as well.

Is this ethical? Am I misrepresenting myself?

Our service is about enhancement, not deception. We work with you to create the best version of yourself, or to visualize aspirational scenarios. We require you to have the rights to the photos you provide and will not create 'deepfakes' or images of other individuals without their consent. Transparency and ethical use are core to our values.
